DietRx is an resource for health impacts of food text-mined from scientific literature integrated with their associations with food chemicals and genes. It facilitates searching associations between food, disease, chemical and gene based on empirical data curated from external repositories and those compiled from text-mining biomedical literature as well as inferred associations.
DietRx comprises of four entities: Food, Disease, Chemical and Gene. The resource integrates evidence of associations among these entities and facilitates in drawing inferences relating them.
DIetRx facilitates an elastic search to query food, disease, chemical and gene. Each of these could be flexibly queries using standard/scientific symbol/names and scientific names. The search results page for each of these entities provides all relevant associations with the remaining entities.
